============================
CAMPAIGN DESCRIPTION
============================

Heritage Life Solutions uses SMS and MMS to communicate with insurance clients and licensed agents across four use cases: (1) Two-factor authentication codes for secure portal login, (2) Customer care replies to policy questions and service requests, (3) Account notifications for policy status changes, payment reminders, and appointment confirmations, (4) Marketing messages for new product offerings, open enrollment periods, and promotional updates. Additionally, Heritage Life Solutions sends policy documents, quotes, and illustrations to clients via MMS and SMS links to PDF documents for convenient mobile review and e-signature. Recipients are existing clients, prospective clients who submit inquiry forms, and contracted insurance agents.


============================
OPT IN WORKFLOW DESCRIPTION
============================

Customers and agents opt in to receive SMS messages through the following methods. Digital: Users provide their mobile phone number on the Heritage Life Solutions website (heritagels.org) during account registration, lead inquiry forms, or quote request forms. Adjacent to the phone number field, disclosure language states: "By providing your phone number, you consent to receive SMS messages from Heritage Life Solutions including two-factor authentication codes, customer care responses, account notifications, and marketing messages. Msg & data rates may apply. Msg frequency varies. Reply STOP to cancel." Users must actively check a consent checkbox before submitting the form. Verbal: Licensed agents may collect verbal opt-in consent during phone consultations, which is documented in the CRM system with a timestamp.
