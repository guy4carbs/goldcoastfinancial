============================
CAMPAIGN DESCRIPTION
============================

Heritage Life Solutions uses SMS and MMS to communicate with insurance clients and licensed agents across four use cases: (1) Two-factor authentication codes for secure portal login, (2) Customer care replies to policy questions and service requests, (3) Account notifications for policy status changes, payment reminders, and appointment confirmations, (4) Marketing and promotional messages for new product offerings, open enrollment periods, and special rates. Additionally, Heritage Life Solutions sends policy documents, quotes, and illustrations to clients via MMS and SMS links to PDF documents for convenient mobile review and e-signature. Recipients are existing clients, prospective clients who submit inquiry forms, and contracted insurance agents.


============================
OPT IN WORKFLOW DESCRIPTION
============================

Heritage Life Solutions collects SMS opt-in consent through two methods:

1. DIGITAL OPT-IN (Website): Users provide their mobile phone number on the Heritage Life Solutions website (heritagels.org) during account registration, lead inquiry forms, or quote request forms. Adjacent to the phone number field, the following disclosure language is displayed: "By providing your phone number and checking this box, you consent to receive SMS and MMS messages from Heritage Life Solutions. Message types include: two-factor authentication codes, customer care responses, account notifications (such as appointment reminders, policy updates, and payment reminders), and marketing/promotional messages (such as new product offerings, special rates, and enrollment updates). Message frequency varies. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out at any time. Reply HELP for help. View our Privacy Policy at heritagels.org/privacy and Terms at heritagels.org/terms." Users must actively check a consent checkbox before submitting the form. The form cannot be submitted without this checkbox selected.

2. VERBAL OPT-IN (Phone): During phone consultations, licensed agents read the following script to the customer: "I'd like to send you text messages from Heritage Life Solutions regarding your account. These messages may include appointment reminders, policy updates, payment reminders, customer support responses, verification codes, and marketing or promotional messages about new products and special rates. Message frequency varies. Standard message and data rates may apply. You can reply STOP at any time to opt out, or reply HELP for assistance. Our privacy policy is available at heritagels.org/privacy. Do you consent to receive these text messages?" The agent records the customer's verbal consent in the CRM system with a timestamp and the agent's name. For marketing messages specifically, a double opt-in is used: after verbal consent is recorded, the system automatically sends a confirmation SMS to the customer stating: "Heritage Life Solutions: You've been opted in to receive promotional messages. Reply YES to confirm or STOP to cancel. Msg & data rates may apply." The customer must reply YES before any marketing messages are sent. This confirmation is logged in the system.
