============================
CAMPAIGN DESCRIPTION
============================

Heritage Life Solutions is a licensed independent insurance agency (DBA of Gold Coast Financial Partners LLC) that uses SMS and MMS to communicate with its insurance clients and licensed agents across four use cases: (1) Two-factor authentication codes for secure portal login, (2) Customer care replies to policy questions and service requests, (3) Account notifications for policy status changes, payment reminders, and appointment confirmations, (4) Marketing and promotional messages for new product offerings, open enrollment periods, and special rates. Additionally, Heritage Life Solutions sends policy documents and quotes to clients via MMS and SMS links to PDF documents for convenient mobile review. All recipients are Heritage Life Solutions' own clients, prospective clients who submit inquiry forms on the Heritage Life Solutions website, and contracted insurance agents.


============================
OPT IN WORKFLOW DESCRIPTION
============================

Heritage Life Solutions collects SMS opt-in consent through two methods:

1. DIGITAL OPT-IN (Website): Users provide their mobile phone number on the Heritage Life Solutions website (heritagels.org). The phone number field is optional on all forms. When a phone number is entered, an SMS consent checkbox appears with the following disclosure: "By checking this box, I agree to receive SMS and MMS messages from Heritage Life Solutions (Gold Coast Financial Partners LLC) including authentication codes, customer care responses, account notifications, and marketing/promotional messages. Message frequency varies. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out or HELP for help. Privacy Policy." The Privacy Policy text links to heritagels.org/legal/privacy. The form can be submitted without a phone number, but if a phone number is provided, the consent checkbox must be checked. This consent language appears on the following public website forms: Contact page (heritagels.org/contact), Get a Quote (heritagels.org/quote), Become an Agent (heritagels.org/agents/become-an-agent), Careers (heritagels.org/careers), and Referral pages. Screenshots of these opt-in forms are available upon request.

2. VERBAL OPT-IN (Phone): During phone consultations, licensed agents read the following script verbatim to the customer: "I'd like to send you text messages from Heritage Life Solutions regarding your account. These messages may include authentication codes for secure login, customer care responses to your questions, account notifications such as appointment reminders and policy updates, as well as marketing and promotional messages about new products and special rates. Message frequency varies. Standard message and data rates may apply. You can reply STOP at any time to opt out, or reply HELP for assistance. Our privacy policy is available at heritagels.org/legal/privacy. Do you consent to receive these text messages?" The agent records the customer's verbal consent in the CRM system with a timestamp, the customer's name, phone number, and the agent's name. For marketing messages specifically, a double opt-in process is required: after verbal consent is recorded, the system automatically sends an initial confirmation SMS to the customer stating: "Heritage Life Solutions: You've been opted in to receive promotional messages. Reply YES to confirm you'd like to receive marketing texts, or reply STOP to cancel. Msg & data rates may apply." The customer must reply YES to this confirmation message before any marketing or promotional messages are sent. If the customer does not reply YES or replies STOP, no marketing messages are sent. This double opt-in confirmation and the customer's response are logged in the system with a timestamp.
