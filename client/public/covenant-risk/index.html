The Covenant Process

Six phases. One structured outcome.

Every engagement follows the Covenant Risk Assessment™ framework.

Phase 01
Discovery
We map your full financial world — income, assets, debt, dependents, existing coverage. We understand your life better than most advisors ever will.
Phase 02
Stress Test
We walk through real-life scenarios — premature death, disability, longevity — and model the financial consequences of each. You see exactly where your plan fails.
Phase 03
Quantification
We assign real dollar figures to your exposure — income replacement, debt obligations, retirement gaps. Abstract fear becomes measurable reality.
Phase 04
Covenant Design
This is where we build your Covenant. A structured plan: If X happens → Y is already handled. Tools are deployed. Products are secondary.
Phase 05
Implementation
We put the structure in place. Application, ownership, beneficiary design, funding strategy. Nothing is left to chance.
Phase 06
Oversight
Annual reviews, life-event adjustments, policy optimization. Your Covenant evolves as your life evolves. This is where we separate from 99% of the industry.

Income Risk

Asset Protection
Wealth isn’t just built — it must be shielded. We identify the legal, financial, and structural vulnerabilities that leave your assets exposed.
Trusts + ownership structuring
Tax Risk
Taxes erode wealth silently. We quantify the long-term impact and deploy strategies that legally minimize exposure across income, estate, and capital gains.
Tax-efficient structuring
Longevity Risk
Living longer than your money is a real scenario. We stress-test your retirement runway and engineer guaranteed income streams that can’t be outlived.

Liability Exposure
Lawsuits, professional malpractice, personal injury claims — one event can unwind decades of wealth. We map the exposure and structure the firewall.
Umbrella + liability structuring
Market Risk
Markets are volatile. Your financial plan shouldn’t be. We insulate critical protection from market exposure so your safety net never depends on timing.
Non-correlated protection
Business Continuity

Buy-sell + key person coverage
